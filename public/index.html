Battle of Yavin

In a galaxy far, far away, the Galactic Empire and Rebel Alliance clash in a decisive battle above the planet Yavin. As a turret operator aboard your faction's mothership, your mission is to defend against incoming enemy fighters.

Game Rules

• Control your turret with Up/Down arrow keys to adjust angle

• Press Spacebar to fire at enemy fighters

• Prevent fighters from breaching your deflector shield - each breach reduces your mothership's health

• Fighters will increase in speed over time, testing your reflexes

• Activate cheat mode with Shift+S (grants shields to your fighters)

Goal

Destroy enemy fighters and reduce the opponent's mothership health to 0 first to claim victory!
